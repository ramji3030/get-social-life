### Q: Why would professionals pay ‚Çπ800+ for a dinner with strangers?

### Q: How do you get traction before launch?
**A**: Waitlist (targeting 1K signups). Pre-launch testing with 50 early users (Hyderabad tech community). Offer free first dinners to build social proof.

### Q: What's your competitive advantage?
**A**: Personality matching algo (cosine similarity on 5 dimensions). Network effects: users rate dinners ‚Üí better matches ‚Üí more retention ‚Üí more supply ‚Üí growth flywheel. Most competitors are event-based, not matching-based.

### Q: Why Hyderabad first?
**A**: 2M+ young professionals, high tech density (TCS, Microsoft, Amazon hubs), low unit costs (dinner ‚Çπ500‚Äì800 vs ‚Çπ1,500+ in Bangalore). Fastest path to profitability.

### Q: How do you monetize?
**A**: B2C (user dinners, premium features), B2B2C (employer partnerships for team building), venue commissions (restaurants get customers), premium matching ($50/month).

### Q: What's your runway with $250K?
